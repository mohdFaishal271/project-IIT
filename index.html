SHERE WITH WORLD

Everything on Earth can be placed into one of four major subsystems: land, water, living things, and air. These four subsystems are called "spheres." Specifically, they are the "geosphere" (land), "hydrosphere" (water), "biosphere" (living things), and "atmosphere" (air).

ROUTES YOU NEVER TOOK

I never journeyed through the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu, marveling at its mystical beauty. The Silk Road's fabled path, connecting civilizations, eluded my steps. The Arctic's icy wilderness remained untrodden, where polar bears roam. I missed the chance to witness the breathtaking serenity of New Zealand's Milford Sound. The bustling streets of Tokyo, pulsating with vibrant energy, were never mine to explore. The Amazon rainforest's hidden wonders, veiled in lush greenery, remained a mystery. And the vast savannas of Africa, home to majestic wildlife, echoed with my absence. Yet, these paths await, calling for future adventures to unfold.
